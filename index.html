Why It Matters

Literacy is the foundation of everything.

Reach Out and Read of Greater New York trains pediatricians to prescribe books and reading aloud at every well-child visit from birth to age 5. Dolly Parton's Imagination Library mails a free book monthly to enrolled children. Together, these programs give every New York child a fair start — and they need the State's support.

👶

Starts at birth

Reading aloud from infancy builds vocabulary, listening skills, and a lifelong love of books — years before formal schooling begins.

🏥

Doctor-delivered

Pediatricians are trusted voices. When they hand a family a book and guidance at check-ups, parents listen — and children thrive.

📬

Books by mail

Dolly Parton's Imagination Library sends age-appropriate books monthly, ensuring every enrolled child has books at home regardless of income.

💸

Proven ROI

Early literacy investment reduces special education costs, grade repetition, and juvenile justice involvement. The payoff for taxpayers is enormous.
